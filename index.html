Rock, Paper, Scissors game.

Rules to play the game ↓

⇨ You will be playing against the computer

⇨ You can choose between rock, paper and scissors

⇨ The first one to get three points wins the game

⇨ Here are the rules to get points in the game:

  • Rock beats scissors
  • Scissors beats paper
  • Paper beats rock

If the player and the computer choose the same option (Ex. Paper and Paper), then no one gets the point.
